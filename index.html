About Me

I am a 20-year-old student from Pakistan who completed FSc and is currently pursuing a BS in Artificial Intelligence while working as a Virtual Medical Assistant. Balancing studies and professional responsibilities has shaped me into someone disciplined, adaptable, and constantly striving to grow. Working in the medical support field has strengthened my communication skills, sense of responsibility, and ability to handle real-world challenges with patience and precision. At the same time, my academic journey in AI reflects my curiosity about the future—about technology, intelligence, and building solutions that actually matter. Beyond academics and work, I am deeply passionate about football, chess, writing shayari, and having meaningful, thought-provoking conversations. I enjoy exploring ideas, understanding people’s stories, and creating things that carry purpose and depth. Whether it’s strategy on the chessboard, creativity in poetry, or teamwork on the football field, I am always drawn to experiences that challenge my mind and character. I believe in building a life that is not just successful, but meaningful—where growth, creativity, and impact go hand in hand.
